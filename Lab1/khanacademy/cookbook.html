Margherita Pizza

Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Pizza Dough 1 (12-inch)
Tomato Sauce 1/2 cup
Fresh Mozzarella 200g (sliced)
Fresh Basil Leaves Handful

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Spread tomato sauce over the pizza dough.
  3. Add slices of mozzarella and fresh basil leaves.
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy!
