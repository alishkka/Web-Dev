About Medeo

Medeo is a famous high-altitude ice rink located near Almaty, Kazakhstan. It is known for its stunning mountain views and ideal conditions for winter sports.

Attractions

Surrounded by the beautiful Tian Shan mountains, Medeo offers breathtaking scenery and fresh air. Visitors can enjoy the panoramic view from the nearby Shymbulak ski resort.

Activities

Visitors can enjoy ice skating in winter, hiking in summer, and taking scenic cable car rides to Shymbulak. Medeo is also a great place for photography and relaxation.

Travel Tips

It is best to visit Medeo in the morning to avoid crowds. Dress warmly in winter, and bring comfortable shoes for hiking in summer. Public transport and taxis are available from Almaty.
